A non-compliant invoice can be rejected by your client and expose you to tax penalties. Here is the complete checklist of mandatory information for flawless Luxembourg invoices.

Checklist of Mandatory Information

According to Article 63 of the Luxembourg VAT Law, your invoice must imperatively contain the following elements:

✅ Issuer Information

  • ☐ Full name or company name
  • ☐ Complete address of registered office
  • ☐ Intra-community VAT number (format LU + 8 digits)
  • ☐ RCS number (if registered company)
  • ☐ Legal form (SARL, SA, etc.)

✅ Client Information

  • ☐ Full name or company name
  • ☐ Complete address
  • ☐ VAT number (mandatory for B2B intra-community)

✅ Invoice Information

  • ☐ Unique invoice number (chronological sequence)
  • ☐ Issue date of the invoice
  • ☐ Delivery or service date (if different)

✅ Detail of Goods or Services

  • ☐ Clear and detailed description
  • ☐ Quantity delivered or provided
  • ☐ Net unit price
  • ☐ Any discounts or rebates

✅ VAT Information

  • ☐ VAT rate applicable per line
  • ☐ VAT amount per rate
  • ☐ Taxable base per VAT rate
  • ☐ Exemption mention if applicable

✅ Totals

  • ☐ Net total (excluding taxes)
  • ☐ Total VAT
  • ☐ Gross total (all taxes included)

Conditional Mentions According to Cases

Reverse Charge (EU Client)

If you invoice a business client in another EU country without VAT:

"VAT Exemption - Article 44 of the law of 12 February 1979 - Reverse Charge"

Export Outside EU

For sales outside the European Union:

"VAT Exemption - Export outside EU"

VAT Franchise

If you benefit from the basic franchise (small business):

"VAT not applicable - Article 60bis of the law of 12 February 1979"

Deposit Invoice

For a deposit invoice, add:

"Deposit on order no. [reference] of [date]"

Credit Note

For a credit note, you must mention:

"Credit note on invoice no. [number] of [date]"

Invoice Numbering

Numbering must follow these rules:

  • Unique: each invoice has a distinct number
  • Chronological: numbers follow in order of issue
  • Without gaps: no breaks in the sequence
  • Non-reusable: a number can only be assigned once

Examples of Accepted Formats

Format Example
Year + number 2026-0001, 2026-0002
Prefix + number INV-001, INV-002
Simple number 00001, 00002

Payment Terms

Although not strictly mandatory, it is recommended to indicate:

  • Payment deadline (e.g., 30 days)
  • Due date
  • Bank details (IBAN, BIC)
  • Applicable late payment penalties

Consequences of a Non-Compliant Invoice

⚠️ Risks Incurred

  • Rejection of VAT deduction by your client
  • Administrative fines from the AED
  • Tax adjustment in case of audit
  • Loss of commercial credibility
